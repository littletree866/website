üÉè Blackjack+

A classic blackjack card game against the dealer. Hit or Stand strategically to reach 21 without busting.

How to Play

Each round, you and the dealer receive 2 cards. Your goal is to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over (busting). Hit to take another card or Stand to keep your total. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10, Aces are 1 or 11.

Controls

  • Hit Button - Take another card (try to get closer to 21)
  • Stand Button - Keep your current hand and let the dealer play
  • Restart Button - Start a new round (shows after game ends)

Features

  • Full 52-card deck with shuffling
  • Dealer AI that follows standard blackjack rules (hits on 16, stands on 17+)
  • Chip counter (starts with 100 chips)
  • 10-chip bet per hand
  • Win/Lose/Push outcomes
  • Visual card display
  • Automatic deck reshuffling when low

Tips & Strategy

  • Always hit on 11 or lower
  • Always stand on 17 or higher
  • If the dealer shows 6 or lower, they're more likely to bust‚Äîbe cautious
  • If the dealer shows 7 or higher, be aggressive and hit until you have 17+
  • Aces can be worth 1 or 11 automatically‚Äîuse this to your advantage
